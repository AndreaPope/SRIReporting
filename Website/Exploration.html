Fund Performance

The dashboard below allows you to further explore how individual funds perform financially. You can filter by fund type (balanced, large cap, etc.) and fund attribute - essentially the category of social responsibility you're interested in using for evaluation (carbon emissions, human rights, etc.). You can also search for specific funds by name.

In the first panel, examine the average return by fund type and years of investment to determine how to maximize your profit. Remember that fund type is often associated with risk level. For example, you would look at Large Cap funds if you want lower risk, and you would see a one year investment provides the highest return.

Another component of investing involves accounting for management fees - the money a financial adviser charges to buy, sell and manage funds on your behalf. The second panel of the dashboard allows you to identify funds that will maximize your return while minimizing your fees.

The third panel of the dashboard gives additional detail on returns based on the fund attribute selected in panel one.
